Inclusive asset sharing platform for small farmers in rural areas

In the world there are approximately 500 million of small farmers without enough resources to acquire agricultural equipment that could empower their activities.

Obsidian combines the lessons learned from different financial services in rural environments and Blockchain technology to create a unique solution that we believe is going to support the development of rural ecosystems.

The Challenge

Designing products and services for rural environments could face different kind of problems, for instance the levels of literacy of the potential customers can make difficult the adoption of new technologies.

In order to get a broader understanding of the people we are designing for. We followed a user-centred approach that help us uncover important insights about the social networks mechanisms and dynamics in rural communities.

The Goal

Empower small farmers around the world by helping them to get access to agricultural equipment in a cheap and affordable way.

Obsidian for Governments

The asset sharing provides a viable strategy, so that governments could achieve a higher number of mechanisation of land with a smaller number of agricultural equipment units.

Obsidian for leasing companies

Our asset sharing model will enable leasing companies to access a price sensitive customer segment and expand their market.
